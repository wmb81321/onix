# Create & Use Accounts

Create and integrate Tempo accounts into your product with the universal Tempo Wallet or domain-bound passkeys.

## What should I use?

### Tempo Wallet

Most apps should use [Tempo Wallet](/accounts/api/dialog) - it provides a universal account experience with a central account for users that includes embedded onramp, access keys, and transaction orchestration out of the box.

### Domain-bound Passkeys

Use [domain-bound passkeys](/accounts/api/webAuthn) when you want to build your own wallet experience or when your app needs to manage and own the WebAuthn ceremony directly.

:::info
Tempo Wallet uses domain-bound passkeys (on `tempo.xyz`) under the hood.
:::

### Other Wallets

Offer users a range of existing wallets to connect from, such as MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, and others.
